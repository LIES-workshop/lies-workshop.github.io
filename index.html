LIES

Language Models, the Information Environment, and Society

An academic workshop bridging LLM research and the social science of information, bias, and democracy.

About

The Workshop on Language Models, the Information Environment, and Society (LIES) brings together researchers from NLP, machine learning, and the social sciences to examine how large language models are reshaping the information environments that underpin democratic society. Topics include political and ideological bias, misinformation and fact-checking, content moderation, epistemic autonomy, civic discourse, and governance.
